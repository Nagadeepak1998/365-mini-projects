Built a small AI/DevOps guardrail project: LLM Runbook Drift Check.

It reviews AI-generated incident runbooks for missing operational actions, weak evidence grounding, unsupported root-cause claims, and risky mitigation advice. The goal is simple: make AI support outputs safer before a team trusts them during production incidents.
