Built a small alert-noise review tool today: `alert-noise-budget-check`.

It reads a saved alert-history snapshot and flags page-heavy alerts that are not producing enough real action, plus repeated pages for the same incident and poor acknowledgement rates.

I like these smaller tools because they turn vague on-call pain into something I can inspect locally with a sample file and a few tests.
